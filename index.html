About Us

With over 34 years of fast and high-quality transportation experience, KRT is a leading transportation service provider with coverage throughout the state, we aim to provide maximum customer satisfaction. Moreover, we not only deliver goods to customers also transportation is taken deliberately on time. Our first customer was Mother India Farming Pvt ltd company, who was satisfied with our services and become our clients. Our commitment towards service made us reach several clients like Shetron limited, Kewaunee scientific corporation, Accupack, neo foods, Indian agro experts, and many other clients.


Mr. Ramaswamy Reddy

The founder: KRT (Sri KodandaRama Transport) was established by Mr. Ramaswamy Reddy in 1986. Initially, it was started with a single-vehicle later it achieved immense growth over the years.
